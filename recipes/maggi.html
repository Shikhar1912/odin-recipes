Maggi

Ingredients

  • Maggi Noodle
  • Maggi Masala
  • Water

Steps

  1. Put water in a pan and let it get hot
  2. When you observe bubbles at the bottom of the pan, add maggi masala
  3. After mixing the masala well, add the noodles
  4. Stir till there is very less water present in the pan
  5. Voila! Eat the maggi right out of the pan
